Is it a Bribe or a Tip?

Justice should be equal . . . right? From the perspective of the elite these are just nothing but a mere joke to them. Would you rather hear "It was only a Tip and a Thanks for letting me get away with what I have done."? I'd rather not, justice isn't about siding on who's got the "Money" or "I'm a Politician", it's about being treated equally cause in the eyes of the law nothing is above it. These "Politicians" are said to be law abiding citizens yet they use their position and their so called "Money" which we all know that its the people's money, the tax that came from the public are used for their personal use and they took advantage of it.

Facts doesn't Lie, but Wealth Prevails

The fact that the Philippine Government is already filled with corruption, many people doesn't get the right treatment they deserve. People who's got the money, the position, the fame are the ones who mostly gets the respect but if you're at the lowest of the low you’re just a nobody to them. Every law abiding citizen here in this country pay taxes, and what are these “Taxes” for? These are collected by the Government to fund public services, let’s say Financial Aid for public schools or welfare programs for the poor, Infrastructure, Agriculture and so on. Yet where are these taxes that came from the people, why aren’t any changes that happened, why are the roads still broken, why does flood keeps on occurring, why does the poor gets poorer? A lot of different question arise from the public and yet not a single truth is unveiled. We, the public, the people of this forsaken country deserves the truth. We are being robbed by the government, why does the justice side with these burglars and liars, are they too afraid to speak up? or are they kept quiet because they were paid by using the citizens money? It’s a big joke to say that these are what they call “JUSTICE”, but do remember this, “There is no secret that cannot be revealed”.
