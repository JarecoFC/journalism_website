Daily Struggles

By Sah

These photograph captures the daily struggles of ordinary Filipinos enduring the congestion of trains, floods in the streets, heavy traffic, and the constant race against time in public transportation.

All of these are part of their fight to provide for their families and to live with dignity.

Yet behind their sweat and sacrifices, the nation remains wounded by persistent corruption.

The taxes that each citizen works hard to pay are not fully utilized for the country's welfare, as a large portion ends up in the pockets of corrupt officials.

Here, the absence of justice becomes evident, while the people endure meager services and heavy burdens, a few continue to indulge in wealth that is not theirs.

These images reflect not only the physical struggles of the people, but also the greater battle for justice and integrity in the nation.
