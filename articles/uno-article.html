Drowned Futures

By uno

These pictures speak of struggle and survival. its show a pedicab driver forcing his way through floodwaters, and students trudging to school in soaked uniforms.

They don't just show rain, they show how ordinary people are left to fight through problems that could have been solved long ago.

The flood says, "I am here because those in power failed you." The tired faces say, "We keep going because we must."

These images don't just capture a moment they reveal a truth: the poor carry the weight of a broken system, while those responsible stay dry.
